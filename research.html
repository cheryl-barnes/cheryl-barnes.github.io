research
I try to make my work open access so that findings from shared resources are available to everyone (academic and agency scientists, fishery managers, stakeholders, students, etc.), without charge. The links below showcase some of my publicly available work. All projects were highly collaborative...so please check out the acknowledgments. This section credits people who were involved in the work (e.g., via study design, data collection, and/or interpretation of results), but not listed as coauthors.

Please also CLICK HERE for a list of interns, research assistants, and technicians who have helped me along the way. Many of them can also be seen in the PHOTO GALLERY.
