research

I try to make my work open access so that findings from shared resources are available to everyone (academic and agency scientists, fishery managers, stakeholders, students, etc.), without charge.

The links below showcase some of my work to date. All projects were highly collaborative. Please check out the acknowledgments sections, which credit those involved in study design, data collection, and/or interpretation of results.
